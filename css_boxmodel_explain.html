CSS Box Model

Name

Element

Definition
Margin
  • margin: 100px 50px 100px 50px;, margin-top: 100px;, margin-right: 50px;, margin-bottom: 100px;, margin-left: 50px;
 The Margin Clears the area outside the border.
Border
  • border-style:; 1px dotted, 1px dashed, 1px solid, 1px double, 1px groove, 1px ridge, 1px inset, 1px outset, 1px none, 1px hidden.
  • For border-style you can also be specific by using border-top-style, border-right-style, border-bottom-style, border-left-style.
 A border goes around the padding and content
Padding
  • padding: 75px 25px 50px 100px; padding-top: 75px;, padding-right: 25px;, padding-bottom: 50px;, padding-left: 100px;
 The Padding clears area around the content.
Content
  • p
  • h1-h6
  • img
 The content is where the image or text is being displayed.
