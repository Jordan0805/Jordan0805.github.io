Due to the fact that he was given military training and was going to be used as a weapon and he knew all of the secrets to his powers he was know free and could do what he wanted.

When Shadowwalker became a threat to someone who was very powerful then needed to hire someone to kill him so then they called the mercenary Tempest he accepted the job with no hesitation because he thought it would be a fun challenge.
